34th Annual Pacific Northwest Sacred Harp Singing Convention - Oregon Session

Please join us in Portland on Saturday and Sunday October 18-19, 2025, for the 34th annual Portland Sacred Harp Singing Convention!

No previous singing experience is required, and newcomers are encouraged to attend.

Registration opens at 9 am. We will sing from 9:30-3:00 both days, with dinner on the grounds at noon.

We will sing from the 2025 Sacred Harp edition (Denson). Loaner books will be available.

Venue

The Laurelhurst Club, 3721 SE Ankeny St, Portland OR

The venue is fully accessible via a ramp from the sidewalk, and there is plenty of nearby on-street parking.

COVID Protocols

We kindly request all attendees to take a Covid test each singing day (including Friday), less than 2 hours prior to entering the venue.

We do have an abundance of Covid tests onsite to share with all singers; please show up early to grab some and test onsite any of the days.

Please stay home if you are ill or have any respiratory symptoms, out of care for each other and especially our elder and immunocompromised singers.

Venue doors and windows will be open to ensure optimal ventilation. Please dress accordingly.

Housing

Please direct inquiries about staying with local singers to poshhousingcommittee@gmail.com. There are also numerous AirBnB's within walking distance; search in the Belmont/Sunnyside, Laurelhurst, and Kerns neighborhoods. Your best hotel options are the Aloft, Holiday Inn Express, and Residence Inn at the airport (PDX), all modern hotels adjacent to MAX light rail stations that are a single easy transfer away from The Laurelhurst Club.
