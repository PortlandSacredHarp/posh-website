12th Annual Portland, Oregon Double All Day Singing

Please join us in Portland on Saturday and Sunday May 2-3, 2026, for our annual Double All Day singing!

We will sing from the Sacred Harp 2025 edition (Denson) on Saturday and the Christian Harmony on Sunday.

The singing will be at the Laurelhurst Club in SE Portland (3721 SE Ankeny St, Portland OR).

No previous singing experience is required, and newcomers are encouraged to attend. Loaner books will be available.

Registration opens at 9 am. We will sing from 9:30-3:00 both days, with dinner on the grounds at noon.

Friday Night Singing School

7-9pm, at the Musician's Union Hall (325 NE 20th Avenue)

Hayden Arp, an incredible longtime singer and friend heralding from Pennsylvania, will teach our singing school on Friday night.

Saturday Evening Social

There will be a social on Saturday evening at the house of a local singer in NE Portland. Address will be provided at the singing.

Housing

If you need a place to stay, please contact our housing chair, Dorothy Mitchell. Email: poshhousingcommittee@gmail.com

Accessibility

The Laurelhurst Club, the main venue for singing, is fully accessible via a ramp from the sidewalk, and there is plenty of nearby on-street parking.

The Musician's Union Hall, the venue for our Friday Singing School, is fully accessible with a small parking lot.
